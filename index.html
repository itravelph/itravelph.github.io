iTravel PH is a GPS based mobile solution that provides public transit routes, attractions and access to local gov't contact info for smart traveling and commuting.

  • Provide different trip and time saving route recommendations for easier ways of commuting
  • Lessen traffic violations by providing commuters an interface for sending reports and complaints to the City Government Authorities
  • Help the City Government in tracking and managing feedbacks from people
  • Assist the tourists as a travel guide to the different tourist attractions through the application.

Features

Trip Plan

Trip Plan module is where the commuters will generate a trip plan by dropping a pin on or searching through the search bar the destination of the commuter in the map.

Attraction

The Attractions module will offer assistance to the tourists by displaying the tourist attractions together with the location, brief information, and routing options if the tourists prefer to visit the place.

File Complaint

File Complaint module is where the users will report the PUV drivers for not following the rules and regulations of the Land Transportation Office (LTO), the Land Transportation Franchising and Regulatory Board (LTFRB) and the Local Government unit (LGU) by the web service through the application to the authorities.
